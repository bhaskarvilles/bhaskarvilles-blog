# Apple WWDC 2025: What to Expect on June 9

Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) 2025 is set to commence on **June 9**, running through **June 13**. The keynote, scheduled for **10:00 a.m. PT (10:30 p.m. IST)**, will be streamed live on [apple.com](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2025/05/apples-worldwide-developers-conference-kicks-off-june-9/), the Apple TV app, and Apple's YouTube channel. This year's conference is expected to showcase significant software updates across Apple's ecosystem.

![](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/images/2025/05/apples-worldwide-developers-conference-kicks-off-june-9/article/Apple-WWDC25-event-details-hero_big.jpg.large_2x.jpg)

Apple is anticipated to introduce **iOS 26**, marking a shift to a year-based naming convention for its operating systems, including **iPadOS 26**, **macOS 26**, **watchOS 26**, **tvOS 26**, and **visionOS 26**. This change aims to provide more flexibility for updates and a unified branding approach across platforms.

## üéß AirPods Enhancements

Significant updates are expected for AirPods:

* **Camera Control**: Users may be able to control an iPhone or iPad's camera by tapping the AirPods' stem to take photos.
* **Sleep Detection**: A feature that pauses audio playback when the AirPods detect the user has fallen asleep, potentially integrating data from the Apple Watch.
* **Head Gestures**: Expanded head gesture controls to accept or reject notifications and calls, enhancing the Conversation Awareness feature.
* **Studio Quality Microphone Mode**: A new mode aiming to provide enhanced background noise reduction, beneficial for content creators.

## ü§ñ Apple Intelligence and AI Features

Building upon last year's introduction of Apple Intelligence, WWDC 2025 is expected to highlight:

* **Enhanced AI Capabilities**: Improvements in battery life management and predictive charging times using AI.
* **Live Translation**: Real-time translation features for AirPods, facilitating seamless communication across languages.
* **Redesigned Shortcuts App**: An updated Shortcuts app with AI-driven suggestions and automation.

Apple is rumored to unveil a new design language, codenamed **Project Solarium**, inspired by visionOS. This includes:

* **Visual Overhaul**: Rounded icons, translucent menus, and a cohesive aesthetic across all platforms.
* **User Interface Consistency**: A unified experience across iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and other operating systems.

## üéÆ New Gaming App

A dedicated gaming application is anticipated, aiming to enhance the gaming experience across Apple's devices. While details are scarce, this move indicates Apple's increased focus on gaming.
